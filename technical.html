Technical Specifications

  • Supported operating modes: Unipolar / Bipolar
  • Fixed-frequency / Dwell Unmodulated / Modulated
  • Output voltage range 4.5 – 28.5 (unipolar)
  • ±4.5 – ±20.5 (bipolar) Volt
  • Frequency range 0.5 – 40 000 Hz
  • Dwell frequency sweep range ±1 %
  • Output current 200 ± 20 mA
  • Frequency range 0.5 – 20 000 Hz
  • Dwell frequency sweep range ±1 %
  • Modulation frequency 5 Hz

Above specs are all in the complete safety range for human and animal cells and because it runs on a square wave form it can generate those low range power packing frequencies that have proven to be more effective than high range sine wave frequencies.

International Safety and Quality Specifications

  • EN 60601-1-2:2001 (Safety requirements for medical electrical systems)
  • EN 55011 (Scientific and Medical Radio-Frequency Equipment)
  • EN 61000-4-2 (Electrostatic Discharge)
  • EN 61000-4-3 (Electromagnetic field immunity test)
  • EN 61000-4-8 (Power Frequency Magnetic Fields)

All the Wave Rife Resonator components carry the necessary internationally certified quality and specification marks. They have passed all their relevant tests and therefore complies with type BF safety requirements. They are safe for human and animal use.

They do not interfere with other electronic equipment.
