Treatments and Frequencies

Understanding Frequencies

Each frequency in our devices is carefully selected based on decades of research and practical application. Our Wave5 Pro Resonator comes with over 800 pre-programmed frequencies targeting various conditions.

Bacterial Infections

  • E. coli: 356.72 Hz
  • Streptococcus: 880-885 Hz
  • Staphylococcus: 727-729 Hz

Viral Conditions

  • Influenza: 440-450 Hz
  • Herpes: 1550-1557 Hz
  • Common Cold: 728-734 Hz

Parasites

  • Roundworms: 440-442 Hz
  • Tapeworms: 164-166 Hz
  • Flukes: 427-429 Hz

Treatment Protocols

For optimal results, we recommend following these general guidelines:

  • Start with lower frequencies and gradually increase
  • Maintain proper hydration during treatments
  • Initial treatments should be 15-30 minutes
  • Advanced users can extend sessions up to 2-3 hours
  • Allow 24-48 hours between intensive treatments
